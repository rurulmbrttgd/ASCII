About PUP Association of Students for Computer Intelligence Integration

Association of Students for Computer Intelligence Integration is the official academic student organization of the Department of Computer Science in the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. AsCII is intended primarily to promote Computer Science as a frontier for creativity and innovation. The enduring purposes of the organization is to give Computer Science newfound respect and recognition, disseminate significant and useful information that contributes to Computer Science, develop learning and a deeper understanding of Computer Science, expand the current knowledge base of every student and promote academic excellence and appreciate culture and arts through various ways of indoctrination of computer-related studies and subjects.

ASCII's Committees

Executives

The Executives Committee is made up of the committee leaders that hold the top positions. It is made up of the Vice President and Assistant Vice President for Communications, Documentations, Marketing, Programs, and Research & Extensions Committee, as well as the President, Vice President Secretary, Assistant Secretary, and Finance.

Communications

The Communications Committee is responsible for developing, updating, and monitoring the organization's communications policies, as well as establishing communication within the organization and outside of the organization to concerned parties.

Documentations

The Documentation Committee is responsible for cataloging, compiling, developing, and updating the organization's activities and documents, as well as provide clear copies of reports thereof.

Marketing

The Marketingg Committee is responsible for generating interest to the organization and ultimately engage in external parties in participating or partnering with the organization.

Programs

The Programs Committee is responsible in directing the actions of the organization to its goals and objectives, as well as planning and coordinating the actions of the organization to concerned parties.

Research & Extensions

The Research and Extensions Committee is responsible for taking feedback for the organization, as well as, study and research pf anu action that may develop the organization and its actions.

ISKODER January 21-25, 2023

An Esports-like tournament that involves coding/programming of students that are taking any computer-related courses. This event consists of 1 versus 1 match-ups of coding, in which a coding problem will be given to the players, and the first player that runs the code perfectly will move on to the next round.
