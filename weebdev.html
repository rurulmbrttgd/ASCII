About Our Team

We are WeebDev, a group of diligent junior students at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS). Our interest in technology and web creation drove us to work together to create a website for PUP ASCII, the official academic organization of PUP's Department of Computer Science. Our goal in creating this website was to raise awareness and attention for the PUP ASCII while also showing our web programming talents and knowledge. We were able to construct a website that truly portrays the vision and ideals of the PUP ASCII while also including modern design elements and functionality because to our hard work and teamwork.
